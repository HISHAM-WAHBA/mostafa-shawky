About Mostafa Shawky

Mostafa Shawky is a talented Egyptian singer who rose to fame through his collaboration with the visionary producer Richard Elhaj . He is known for his unique "Sawi" style that blends traditional Egyptian sounds with modern beats.

His breakthrough hit "Malteshet El 2loub" became a massive success across the Arab world, showcasing his powerful voice and charismatic performance. Since then, he has released a string of successful hits like "Abouya Wassany" and "Dareb 3ilewi".

With a perfect mix of humor, energy, and musicality, Mostafa Shawky has carved a unique niche for himself in the contemporary Arabic music scene, becoming one of the most sought-after artists for concerts and festivals.
